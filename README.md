# Life Expectancy Analysis

This project analyzes countries and their life expectancy against other metrics such as alcohol abuse and diet.

## Tools Used
  - Python
  - Pandas
  - Matplotlib
  - Jupyter Notebook

## Questions Explored
  - Which countries have the highest and lowest life expectancies?
  - Does diet affect life expectancy
  - Is there a correlation between alcohol abuse and life expectancy?

## Summary
  A strong correlation was found between diet and life expectancy. Less developed countries have a lower life expectancy while more developed countries have a higher life expectancy. However, a correlation couldnt be found between alcohol abuse and life expectancy.
